# Subprocessors and external data flows

Potential processors/integrations are Supabase (auth/database/storage), Vercel (hosting/functions/logs), OpenAI (selected AI context), Meta/WhatsApp, Google, Microsoft, Mollie and Resend. A provider receives data only when its module and credentials are configured.

Before production, document for each provider: legal entity, purpose, categories, regions, retention, DPA, transfer mechanism, security contacts, deletion procedure and whether model training is disabled. The repository must not claim contractual or residency guarantees that have not been verified by the operator.
